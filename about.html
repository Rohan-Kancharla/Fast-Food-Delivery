What is the secret receipe of our burgers

Seasoning the burger well provides big flavor and that perfect bite.
Cooking the burger on high heat is a must.
The grill or pan should be at 600 to 800 degrees Fahrenheit,
and a good way to test is by using a bit of vegetable oil,
which starts to smoke at 400-500 degrees. If using a pan, use cast iron.

  • 1 pound ground beef (I prefer 80/20)
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3 Tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
